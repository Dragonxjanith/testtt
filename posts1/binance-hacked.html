Binance blockchain network loses millions of dollars due to hacker attack

Binance can be known as the world's largest Crypto Currency exchange. However, on the 6th, a group of hackers stole BNB Tokens worth about 570 million dollars from Binance.

Last Friday, Binance calculated that between $100 and $110 million worth of tokens had been stolen by hackers. Because of this, the operation of the affected Blockchain has been stopped for a period of eight hours.

Later, the company stated that the hackers had stolen about 02 million BNB Cryptocurrency. The value of one BNB Cryptocurrency is approximately $284. Bina This hacker attack has been carried out targeting the BSC Token Hub, which is a bridge between two nce systems.

Changpeng Zhao, the founder and CEO of Binance, posted on his official Twitter account that the currency is now safe and apologizes for the inconvenience.

Also, in their blog post, it is further stated that hackers were able to steal Binance Cryptocurrency due to a bug in the Bridge Smart Contract. Also, according to them, due to this theft, they have lost about 100 million dollars in a way that they cannot get back.
