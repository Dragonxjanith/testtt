Twitter is working to provide the ability to edit Tweets

You may remember last April, Twitter company officially announced that they hope to provide the ability to edit Tweets. Accordingly, they had indicated that they were going to give this to their Twitter Blue Lab users and test it.

According to the Twitter post posted by Twitter Blue's official Twitter account yesterday, the Twitter company has made arrangements to officially provide Tweet Editing facility for their Blue Lab users. However, the ability to edit Tweets like this is only available in Canada, Australia and New Zealand. They have also indicated that this facility will be provided to the United States in the next few daysNawa
