Meta company introduces a professional mode where you can earn money from your Facebook profile.

Meta Company has introduced a Professional Mode where qualified Creators can earn money for their Facebook profile without creating a Facebook Page.

Mark Zuckerberg recently announced in a Facebook post that Meta has invested about one billion dollars in Creators.



New features in this Professional mode for Facebook Profile

You may know that you can see the Insights of posts and other content of a Facebook Page, you will get this facility after activating this Professional Mode.



One of the first money making options here is the Reels Play bonuses program. Eligible Creatos have the number of Views of their Eligible Content It is possible to earn up to $35,000 per month. Interested Creators can register to get started and the knowledge required to learn how to make Reels is also provided. Along with this, there are plans to expand Facebook Reels to other countries next year.

After enabling this mode on your profile, anyone can follow your profile. They can see your posts in their feed. However, you can choose whether your posts are only for your friends or public.



Meta has stated that this new mode will be tested with limited users in America and based on the results, more changes and bug fixes will be made in the future.

It has been announced that this has been extended to other countries by the Meta company. It also includes Sri Lanka.
